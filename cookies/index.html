Cookie Policy

Last updated: 5 May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how this Site uses them, and how you can control them. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to site owners. Cookies cannot harm your device and do not contain viruses or malware.

How We Use Cookies

This is a static site. We do not set any cookies ourselves. All cookies present on this site are set automatically by Vercel, our hosting provider, as part of their infrastructure. These are all essential cookies required for the site to function and be served correctly.

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any cookies that track you across other websites.

How to Control Cookies

You have several options for controlling cookies:

Browser settings — Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the site work.

You can also opt out of Vercel Analytics by enabling the "Do Not Track" setting in your browser. Vercel respects this signal.

Your Consent

All cookies on this site are essential infrastructure cookies set by Vercel. Because none of them are analytics, advertising, or non-essential cookies, there is nothing to opt in or out of on our end.

If you wish to block or delete these cookies, you can do so through your browser settings. Note that doing so may affect how the site loads or behaves.

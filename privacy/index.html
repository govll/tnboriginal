Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The New Buckingham website ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects any information when you visit this site. This site is a fan and promotional website hosted via Vercel. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone.

What Information We Collect

This is a static promotional website. We do not operate user accounts, collect form submissions, or process payments. The information that may be collected is limited to:

  • Anonymous usage data collected automatically by Vercel's hosting infrastructure (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time of visit)
  • Cookie and analytics data if you have consented to cookies (see our Cookie Policy)
  • Any information you voluntarily provide if you contact us directly via email

How We Use Your Information

Any data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Understanding how visitors use the site so we can improve it
  • Diagnosing technical issues with the site
  • Responding to direct enquiries

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

Hosting and Third Parties

This website is hosted on Vercel and its source code is stored on GitHub. Both services may collect technical data as part of their standard infrastructure operations. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies:

  • Vercel Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Privacy Statement

We have no control over data collected by these third-party services and are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Cookies

We may use cookies to understand site traffic and improve your experience. You can control cookie preferences at any time. For full details, please read our Cookie Policy.

Links to Other Websites

This site may contain links to external websites. Once you leave our site, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reading the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any data held by Vercel or GitHub as part of their infrastructure is subject to their own retention policies.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

Because this is a static site with no user accounts or databases, the majority of any personal data (such as server logs and IP addresses) is held by Vercel as part of their infrastructure, not by us. For requests relating to that data, you will need to contact Vercel directly.

For data held by Vercel (logs, analytics, IP addresses): privacy@vercel.com

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
